ODIN RECIPES

  • NIHARI

    INGREDIENTS
    1. Meat (Beef or Mutton)
    2. Nihari Masala (Spice Blend)
    3. Ghee or Oil
    4. Onions
    5. Ginger and Garlic Paste

  • BIRYANI

    INGREDIENTS
    1. Noodles (typically egg noodles or rice noodles)
    2. Oil
    3. Vegetables (such as carrots, cabbage, bell peppers)
    4. Garlic
    5. Soy Sauce

  • WHITE SAUCE NOODLES

    INGREDIENTS
    1. Basmati Rice
    2. Meat (Chicken, Mutton, or Beef)
    3. Biryani Masala
    4. Onions
    5. Yogurt
